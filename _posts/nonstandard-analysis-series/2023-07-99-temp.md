### $\b{\omega} \times \b{\Omega} \in \mathbb{U}$

$$
\abs{^* 1} < \abs{\b{\omega}}
\text{ and } 
\abs{^* x} < \abs{\b{\Omega}}
\quad \implies \quad 
\abs{^* 1} \cdot \abs{^* x} < \abs{\b{\omega}} \times \abs{\b{\Omega}}
\quad \implies \quad 
\abs{^* x} < \abs{\b{\omega} \times \b{\Omega}}
$$
